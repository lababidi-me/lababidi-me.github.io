Artist Statement

This project is an exploration into visual elements in photography. It almost attempts to investigate fundamental components that produce a photo. I will incorporate shadow, rotted objects, paper backgrounds, wood backgrounds, different lighting conditions, and possibly different locations. Technically, I’ll mostly stick to large format negatives. I may find a way to juxtapose bw and color images in a way that may not be complementary but may be varying in perspective. The obvious presentation is to take the large format color negative and present it as a large print. I also would like to see how small prints and zoomed in components play into the presentation. I also would like to put together a web page and or a book if I have enough material. With the black and white, I may try to take contact prints and see how they play into the set.
